    create_transaction(attrs)

    View Source

    Creates a transaction.

    Debits the buyer's credit by the widget price and credits the seller's by the same price minus a 5% marketplace fee.

    Examples 

    iex> create_transaction(%{field: value})
{:ok, %Transaction{}}

iex> create_transaction(%{field: bad_value})
{:error, any()}
